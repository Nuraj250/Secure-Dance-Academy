# Out of Scope

The following are explicitly excluded from the Task 02 requirements baseline:

- Multi-academy tenancy
- Payment processing
- Native mobile applications
- AI assistant features
- Public marketing pages
- Advanced analytics beyond operational reporting
- Social media integration
- Direct self-service public registration

## Out of Scope Notes

- Excluded items may be considered in future phases only after approval.
- Exclusion here does not prevent future design decisions from keeping the system
  extensible.
