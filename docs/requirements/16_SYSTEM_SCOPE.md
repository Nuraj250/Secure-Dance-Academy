# System Scope

The system covers secure management of:

- User accounts and roles
- Artist, parent, and coach relationships
- Attendance records
- Performance records
- Injury records
- Medical information
- Emergency contacts
- Activities
- Notifications
- Reports
- Dashboard views
- Audit logs
- Search, filtering, pagination, and export
- Profile management and settings

The scope is limited to a single-academy baseline for Task 02.

## Scope Notes

- Child artist data is treated as sensitive and access-restricted.
- Adult artists may have direct access to their own permitted data.
- Administrative oversight and auditability are included.
