# Success Criteria

- Every stakeholder has a defined role and concern set.
- Every important feature has a measurable requirement.
- Security, privacy, and legal obligations are explicit.
- Business rules are consistent and enforceable.
- Traceability exists from requirements to later verification work.
- The requirements package is ready for architecture work without ambiguity.
- The scope remains stable enough for later design and testing phases.

## Success Criteria Notes

- Success is based on clarity, completeness, and traceability rather than code volume.
